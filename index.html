How to Roll Dice

To have Piss Goblin roll dice, simply enter /roll followed by dice values.

/roll dX: Rolls a X-sided die.
/roll NdX: Rolls N X-sided dice.
/roll NdX + K: Rolls N X-sided dice and adds K to the result.
/roll NdX + K + dY: Rolls N X-sided dice, adds K, and rolls a Y-sided die.

Examples:

/roll d20: Rolls a 20-sided die.
/roll 2d6: Rolls 2 6-sided dice.
/roll 4d10 + 2: Rolls 4 10-sided dice and adds 2 to the result.
/roll 3d8 + 1 + d4: Rolls 3 8-sided dice, adds 1, and rolls a 4-sided die.
