How to Roll Dice

To have Goblin roll dice, simply enter /roll followed by dice values.

/roll dX: Rolls a X-sided die.
/roll NdX: Rolls N X-sided dice.
/roll NdX + K: Rolls N X-sided dice and adds K to the result.
/roll NdX + K + dY: Rolls N X-sided dice, adds K, and rolls a Y-sided die.

Examples:

/roll d20: Rolls a 20-sided die.
/roll 2d6: Rolls 2 6-sided dice.
/roll 4d10 + 2: Rolls 4 10-sided dice and adds 2 to the result.
/roll 3d8 + 1 + d4: Rolls 3 8-sided dice, adds 1, and rolls a 4-sided die.

Other D&D Commands

/help: Displays a guide for commands and a link to this site.
/classes: Displays a list of D&D classes.
/races: Displays a list of D&D races.
/rollStats: Rolls for 6 random ability scores for D&D.
/generate Name: Generates a random name.
/generate character: Generates a random character, providing a Race, Class, Background, and name.

Miscellaneous Commands

/cool guy: A Cool Guy™.
/scream: Goblin will provide you with a High Quality Scream.
/show me goblins: See for yourself.
/show me pigeons: Pigeons.
/addCommand command|message: Creates a command that displays a custom message.
/commands: see a list of commands created by /addCommand. These can sometimes be deleted when the app updates.

New commands will be added over time!
