How to Roll Dice

To have Piss Goblin roll dice, simply enter /roll followed by dice values.
/roll dX: Rolls a X-sided die. /roll NdX: Rolls N X-sided dice. /roll NdX + K: Rolls N X-sided dice and adds K to the result. /roll NdX + K + dY: Rolls N X-sided dice, adds K, and rolls a Y-sided die.
